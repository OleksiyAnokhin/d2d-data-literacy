On June 6, 2019 the D2D team in Mongolia organized the traditional water and mining industry round table, inviting representatives and the senior management of the biggest mining companies and government agencies.

Oleksiy Anokhin presented insights from the EITI datasets, which boosted an active discussion among participants.
