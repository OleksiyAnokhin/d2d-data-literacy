Welcome to the D2D Natural Resources and Data Literacy project page.

We developed this web-site to summarize all information and materials about the D2D work for your convenience. The project contains open data about natural resources and different deliverables (links, posts, pictures, videos, presentations etc).

Let us briefly introduce the D2D program to you:

Program objectives: to improve utility of natural resource industry data for the development impact

Structure: joint WB/IFC program led by IFC

Funding: $2.5 million over 2 years

What do we hope to achieve? Move the needle to enable the various stakeholders to effectively re-use NR data for purposes that are relevant to them
