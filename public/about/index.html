About

Program objectives: to improve utility of natural resource industry data for development impact

Structure: joint WB/IFC program led by IFC

Funding: $2.5 million over 2 years

What do we hope to achieve? Move the needle to enable the various stakeholders to effectively re-use NR data for purposes that are relevant to them
